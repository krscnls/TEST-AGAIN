Noli Me Tangere Characters

Image Character Description
María Clara - María Clara is the fiancée of the main protagonist of the book. She is Kapitan Tiago's daughter. Maria is the idol of the people, she is shown to grow up and be a lovely lady with a nice smile.
Crisostomo Ibarra - Crisostomo Ibarra is the main protagonist of Noli me Tangere. He studied in europe and went back home after 7 years. Upon returning he has found out that his father died from accidentally killing a Spanish tax collector. He is the fiancée of Maria Clara.
Kapitan Tiago - Kapitan Tiago is the father of Maria Clara. When Ibarra came back to the Philippines, Tiago hosted a party for Ibarra for his returnal.
Doña Pia Alba - Dona is the wife of Kapitan Tiago and mother of Maria Clara. After finding out she couldn't get pregnant, she tried various ways to get pregnant by doing religious practices. Although unfortunately she got pregnant by Padro Damaso, which caused her death.
Teniente Guevarra - Teniente Guevarra is a close friend of Ibarra's father, Don Rafael, and was the one who told Ibarra about his fathers death upon his arrival. Guevarra had high respects of Don Rafael, which gave him the decision to protect his son, Crisostomo Ibarra, once Crisostomo comes back home from Europe.
